Meow Bot:

Meow Bot is a Rasa-powered chatbot designed to provide users with interesting cat facts, engage in cat-related conversation, and offer responses to various user interactions.

Features:

Cat Facts: Ask Meow Bot for a cat fact and it will fetch a random cat fact for you.
Greeting: Meow Bot greets users with a friendly "Meow!" and is ready to assist them.
Bot Identity: Ask Meow Bot about its identity, and it will proudly proclaim itself as a cat bot.
Emotional Responses: Meow Bot responds appropriately to expressions of happiness and sadness from users.
